College Tuititon-Salary Explorer

This interactive tool helps you explore the profitability of attending colleges across the United States by comparing tuition costs and early-career salaries.

How to Use This Tool

  • Use the dropdown menus to filter by state, institution size, and in-state vs. out-of-state tuition.
  • Use the search bar to search for a specific school.
  • Hover over points in the scatterplot or bars in the scrolling bar chart to view details. Click to select a college and compare it directly to others. Selected schools will appear in a panel to the right.
  • Scroll down the page to explore the animated bar chart. This scrollable bar chart shows the profitability (Salary-Tuition) for each institution. Click and drag the gray bar at the bottom of the visualization to scroll across schools.
  • Press Esc to clear search selections. To reset filters, select "All" from the dropdown.
